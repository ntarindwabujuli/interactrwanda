OUR BACKGROUND

Over the past times a lot of people have been asking us how Interact Rwanda started, so we thought we would dedicate a page to tell our story. The idea of Interact Rwanda started in 2019., Interact Rwanda was founded by Julius MUGABO a health care provider who was also passionate about making networking. Some people start their own business with a great concept or idea, Julius wasn’t one of these people since for his case it’s something that started small until developed into a big company he owns today.

One day he sat down and asked himself any contribution he could offer to network people, for them to share experience, opportunities and knowledge. He created a small group of fellow guys who used to sit down at certain coffee shops had a coffee together and could share trending news and opportunities in different careers and regions. This was in 2019, but the idea was a bit disturbed by COVID-19 outbreak . Since physical meeting were very limited, with in that one gap year Julius, decided to start various online class courses for events management regardless of his full-time hospital tight schedule, he said “events management is more of passion to me, than a work”.

He had two Jobs by then, and had to quit one part-time Job to go for events management classes, after completion of those online classes, since there was a bit Covid-flexibility measures in events industry he started volunteering at different event management companies during weekends and evenings where he was among organizing committees for various trending corporate events in Kigali, he also volunteered at his friend’s personal events. He later decided to open his own company in Early 2022 in names of Interact Rwanda. From there he learned a lot from starting at the ground and working his way up, until today the company is fully functional, expanding big its projects and operations with an eagerness to bring client’s brand /theme to life.
