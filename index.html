Services

We specialize in event management and are dedicated to creating memorable experiences for our clients. From corporate events and weddings to product launches and charity fundraisers, we have the expertise to bring your vision to life. Our team of experienced event planners will work with you every step of the way, from the initial concept and design to execution and post-event follow-up. We take care of all the details, so you can focus on enjoying your event and making lasting memories. Whether you're planning a small gathering or a large-scale event, we're here to help. Contact us today to learn more about our services and how we can help you make your next event a success.

F.A.Q

We understand that planning an event can be overwhelming, and we want to ensure that our clients have all the information they need to make informed decisions. Below are some of the frequently asked questions we receive from our clients.

  • Why hiring Interact Rwanda event’s planner?

    * We will save yourself from the last-minute stress and worry.
    * Because you need to make the most out of your budget, we will help you stick on it.
    *Sometimes you don’t know where to start and how to make your event a memorable one.
    * Incredible organization skills,
    *Gain from our connections we work with on a daily basis.
    * We know what not to do (the pitfalls in event) and work on avoiding them.

  • What are your areas of expertise? ?

    *Conferences
    *Seminars
    *Team building events
    * Year-End functions
    *Product launch
    * Charity events
    * Happy hours
    *Trade shows
    *Competition / Award ceremonies Corporate dinners

  • What are our fees?

    We are always flexible and we work on our client’s budget and plan accordingly. We always discuss the event's requirements before giving quotations since our most fair fees depend on the nature of the event, complexity of planning, the innovation and our level of involvement.

  • Who handles my event?

    We have a team of event planners, event designers and event managers to meet your every need, it will always depend on the required service.

  • When it comes to event do you do it all?

    *Sadly, currently we aren't a good source if you are just looking to rent décor, Instruments, venues, furniture and so on, we are just an event planning and managing company not a rental company.
    *Due to our specificity, target market and highest demand we choose to reserve ourselves for corporate events only per now.
